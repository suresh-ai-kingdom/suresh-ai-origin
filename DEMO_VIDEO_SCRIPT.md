## üé§ RECORDING TIPS

### Voiceover:
- **Tone:** Confident, friendly, energetic
- **Pace:** Moderate (not too fast, not too slow)
- **Mic:** Use good USB microphone (Blue Yeti or similar)
- **Background:** Quiet room, no echo
- **Practice:** Read script 3-5 times before recording

### Screen Recording:
- **Tool:** OBS Studio (free) or Loom
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 @ 60fps
- **Cursor:** Show mouse cursor, highlight clicks
- **Zoom:** Zoom in on important UI elements
- **Transitions:** Smooth cuts, no jarring jumps

### Editing:
- **Software:** DaVinci Resolve (free) or CapCut
- **Music:** Upbeat background music (from YouTube Audio Library)
- **Volume:** Music at 20%, voiceover at 100%
- **Captions:** Add subtitles for accessibility
- **Export:** 1080p MP4, H.264 codec
