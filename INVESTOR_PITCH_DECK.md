## APPENDIX: KEY METRICS DEFINITIONS

**ARR** (Annual Recurring Revenue): Normalized annual value of subscriptions  
**MRR** (Monthly Recurring Revenue): Monthly subscription revenue  
**CAC** (Customer Acquisition Cost): Cost to acquire one customer  
**LTV** (Lifetime Value): Total revenue from one customer  
**CAGR** (Compound Annual Growth Rate): Average YoY growth  
**EBITDA**: Earnings before interest, tax, depreciation, amortization  
**Gross Margin**: (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue  
**Churn Rate**: % customers lost per month  
**NPS** (Net Promoter Score): Customer satisfaction metric

**Target Metrics:**
- CAC Payback: < 9 months
- LTV/CAC: > 5x
- Gross Margin: 75-85%
- Monthly Churn: < 2%
- NPS: > 50
