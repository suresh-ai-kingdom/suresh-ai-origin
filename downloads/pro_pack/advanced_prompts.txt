ADVANCED AI PROMPTS – PRO LEVEL

----------------------------------
1. BUSINESS IDEA GENERATOR
----------------------------------
Prompt:
"You are a startup strategist.
Generate 10 low-cost online business ideas that can be started in India with zero investment.
For each idea, include:
- Target user
- Problem
- Simple solution
- Monetization method"

----------------------------------
2. CONTENT CREATION MACHINE
----------------------------------
Prompt:
"Act as a professional content creator.
Create a 30-day content plan for Instagram focused on AI, automation, and productivity.
Include hooks, captions, and CTA."

----------------------------------
3. PROBLEM SOLVER PROMPT
----------------------------------
Prompt:
"I am facing this problem: [describe problem].
Break it down step-by-step.
Explain root cause.
Give 3 practical solutions.
Suggest tools or automation if possible."

----------------------------------
4. MONEY THINKING PROMPT
----------------------------------
Prompt:
"Think like a digital entrepreneur.
How can I convert my skills, time, or knowledge into a simple digital product under ₹99?
Give examples."

----------------------------------
5. DAILY AI ASSISTANT PROMPT
----------------------------------
Prompt:
"Be my daily AI assistant.
Help me plan my day, prioritize tasks,
remove distractions, and focus on income-generating work."

Use these prompts daily.
Consistency = Results.
